Nursing Specialties

  • Critical Care Nursing: Focuses on patients with life-threatening conditions in intensive care units.
  • Pediatric Nursing: Provides care for infants, children, and adolescents.
  • Oncology Nursing: Supports patients undergoing cancer treatment.
  • Geriatric Nursing: Cares for elderly patients with chronic conditions.
  • Emergency Nursing: Handles urgent and critical cases in emergency departments.
  • Surgical Nursing: Assists in surgical procedures and patient recovery.
