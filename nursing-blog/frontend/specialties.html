Nursing Specialties

Explore diverse career paths and find your perfect nursing specialty

Critical Care

$75,000 - $95,000

Provide intensive care for critically ill patients in ICU, CCU, and emergency settings. Requires advanced assessment skills and ability to work under pressure.

  • • ICU/CCU experience required
  • • CCRN certification preferred
  • • Advanced life support training
  • • High-stress environment

Pediatric

$65,000 - $85,000

Care for infants, children, and adolescents. Requires specialized knowledge of pediatric development and family-centered care approaches.

  • • Pediatric experience preferred
  • • CPN certification available
  • • Family communication skills
  • • Emotional resilience

Emergency

$70,000 - $90,000

Provide rapid assessment and treatment in emergency departments. Handle trauma, acute illnesses, and emergency situations with quick decision-making.

  • • Emergency experience required
  • • CEN certification preferred
  • • Trauma training
  • • Fast-paced environment

  • • CNOR certification available
